Kerala Chicken Curry

Ingredients

Marination

  • 500g chicken
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt as needed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala (store bought)
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed black pepper

Gravy

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup onions chopped finely (1 large)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes (pureed or chopped and is optional)
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk (thin milk)

Tempering (optional)

  • 1 1/2 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 1 dried red chilli (break and remove the seeds)
  • 3 to 4 pearl onions sliced thinly (or shallots)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
