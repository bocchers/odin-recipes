Kerala Chicken Curry

Ingredients

Marination

  • 500g chicken
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt as needed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala (store bought)
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed black pepper

Gravy

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup onions chopped finely (1 large)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes (pureed or chopped and is optional)
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk (thin milk)

Tempering (optional)

  • 1 1/2 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 1 dried red chilli (break and remove the seeds)
  • 3 to 4 pearl onions sliced thinly (or shallots)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with chilli powder, salt, turmeric, pepper and garam masala. Keep aside until you prepare the rest.
  2. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoon coconut oil in a pan. Add onions and fry until they turn golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and fry until the raw smell goes away, for 1 to 2 mins.
  4. Transfer the marinated chicken and fry on a slightly high flame until it turns white in color, for about 3 to 4 mins
  5. Then add the ground spice mixture and mix well. Cover and cook on a low flame until the chicken is slightly tender. Keep stirring in between for even cooking.
  6. Optional - If using tomatoes, then add at this stage and saute further until tomatoes lose the raw flavour.
  7. If the pan is too dry, pour 1/4 cup hot water. This depends on how much moisture your chicken releases.
  8. Once the chicken is tender, pour the coconut milk. Mix well. If using canned coconut milk, shake it well and then use.
  9. When the coconut milk begins to bubble cover and simmer for 5 mins. Taste test and add more salt.s
